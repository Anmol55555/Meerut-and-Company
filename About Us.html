Who We Are



Welcome to Meerut & Company, your number one source for all your fancy costumes need.

Founded in 2000 by Mr. Vikash Chandra Kesarwani, Meerut & Company has come a long way from its beginnings. When our founder had first started out, his passion for easy availibility of fancy dresses for everyone drove him to built this service so that Meerut & Company can offer you easily available costumes at an offordable price so that no kid feel left out in his Fancy Dress Competitions. We now serve customers all over city, and are thrilled that we'll be able to help you.

We at Meerut & Company have the huge collection of Fancy costumes for everyone, especially for kids. Moreover we provide these costumes and accessories on sale as well as on rent.

Customers can take costumes on rent in a very simplified manner. Be it Regional dresses, Mythological Characters, Themes, Animal, Freedom Fighter dresses or any other category. We also have large variety of costumes with various accessories like hats, wigs and many others.

Our expertise in this domain, for decades, has made us a distinct name in the Prayagraj region. Our success can be attributed to the guardianship of our founder, Vikash Chandra Kesarwani.His contributions in this feild has made us what we are today.
